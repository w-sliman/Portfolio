Hi everyone!

In Sep 2022, armed with a degree in Mathematics (specializing in Mathematical Analysis), a strong technical background, familiarity with multiple programming languages, and a good understanding of structured databases, I decided to make a career shift into the fascinating realm of data.

I made this decision because I know beyond a doubt that I can and will excel in this field. I am confident that building a successful career in data will not only improve the quality of my life but also the lives of those I care about.

I prepared a well-thought-out plan that started with acquiring three professional certificates:

  1. Google Data Analytics.
  2. IBM Data Analysis.
  3. Machine Learning Specialization.

Additionally, I completed the capstone projects for the first two certificates and participated in the Titanic competition on Kaggle, showcasing the knowledge I acquired from the third course through a detailed notebook.

I am proud to share that I achieved all of the above despite enduring challenging circumstances, including a devastating economic situation and having access to electricity for only three hours a day. I continue to maintain the same level of dedication, if not more, as I progress further in my career path.

Now, I am ready to take the next step, with multiple goals in mind:

  1. Continuously learning and improving my knowledge and experience.
  2. Over-delivering and providing the highest ROI for those who invest in me.
  3. Being there for the people I can help, whether at work or outside of it.

I am a native Arabic speaker, fluent in English, and currently learning French (at a B1 level). I am also looking forward to learning more languages in the future.

Excitement fills me as I approach the next phase of my career path. I am eager to get to know the people I will be working with, to learn from them, share my knowledge, and become a strong pillar in achieving their success.

While there is still more I'd like to share, I must keep this section concise. The door is open for you to reach out to me. Let's have a conversation! Feel free to send me an email or a direct message on LinkedIn. I would be delighted to talk to you.
